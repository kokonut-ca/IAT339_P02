ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Chaleur specialises in the retail of fragrances perfect for your home that are well suited to different occasions and moods. We carefully select the best diffusers, candles, refills, tea lights and many other products from a select few of the finest manufacturers within Canada. Whether you are looking for a warming Christmas-scented candle, or an exotic scent diffuser to fill the room in the summer, we are confident that you will find exactly what you are looking for here at Chaleur.

TWO MAIN PRODUCTS

Experience the beneficial properties of essential oils by diffusing them into the air you breathe with our diffusers designed for a spectrum of spaces and places.

All our candles are made using 100% natural vegetable-based wax. A mixture of soy and rapeseed, the wax is sustainable and made from renewable sources.
